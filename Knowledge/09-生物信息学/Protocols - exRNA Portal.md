# Protocols - exRNA Portal

> source: [exrna.org](https://exrna.org/resources/protocols/)

## 核心要点

1. A major goal of ERCC Stage 1 was to standardize these procedures to minimize experimental variation across labs and experiments
2. Most of the protocols were updated after first release in 2015 based on vetting the originals in multiple labs
3. The major change to isolation and enrichment protocols was an increase in input volume of biofluid from 200 µL to 500 µL
