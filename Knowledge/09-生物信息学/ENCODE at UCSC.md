# ENCODE at UCSC

> source: [genome.ucsc.edu](http://genome.ucsc.edu/ENCODE/)

## 核心要点

1. ENCODE results from 2007 and later are available from the 
ENCODE Project Portal,
encodeproject.org
2. This covers data generated during the two production phases 2007-2012
and 2013-present
3. UCSC coordinated data for the ENCODE Consortium from its inception in 2003 (Pilot phase) 
to the end of the first 5 year phase of whole-genome data production in 2012
