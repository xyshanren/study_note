# Chromosomes Fact Sheet

> source: [www.genome.gov](http://www.genome.gov/26524120)

## 核心要点

1. Chromosomes are thread-like structures located inside the nucleus of animal and plant cells
2. Each chromosome is made of protein and a single molecule of deoxyribonucleic acid (DNA)
3. Passed from parents to offspring, DNA contains the specific instructions that make each type of living creature unique
