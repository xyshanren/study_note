# Cistrome Cancer

> source: [cistrome.org](http://cistrome.org/CistromeCancer/)

## 核心要点

1. Cistrome Cancer is a comprehensive resource for predicted transcription factor (TF) targets and enhancer profiles in cancers
2. The prediction was from integrative analysis of TCGA expression profiles and public ChIP-seq profiles
3. The target prediction is based on integration of expression correlation over the samples in each cancer from TCGA and TF binding patterns in the genome from public ChIP-seq data
