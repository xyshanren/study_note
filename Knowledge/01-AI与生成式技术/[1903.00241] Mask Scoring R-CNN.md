# [1903.00241] Mask Scoring R-CNN

> source: [arxiv.org](https://arxiv.org/abs/1903.00241)

## 核心要点

1. In the task of instance segmentation, the confidence of instance classification is used as mask quality score in most instance segmentation frameworks
2. However, the mask quality, quantified as the IoU between the instance mask and its ground truth, is usually not well correlated with classification score
3. In this paper, we study this problem and propose Mask Scoring R-CNN which contains a network block to learn the quality of the predicted instance masks
