# Functional Observational Battery (FOB)

> source: [www.mds-usa.com](http://www.mds-usa.com/safetytox_fob.html)

## 核心要点

1. This battery of tests is not intended to provide a detailed evaluation of neurotoxicity
2. It is designed to be used in conjunction with neuropathologic evaluation and/or general toxicity testing
3. Additional functional tests may be necessary to assess completely the neurotoxic potential of a chemical
