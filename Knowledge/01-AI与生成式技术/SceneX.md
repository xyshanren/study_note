# SceneX

> source: [zhouzq1.github.io](https://zhouzq1.github.io/SceneX/)

## 核心要点

1. Recently, Procedural Controllable Generation (PCG) has gained significant attention in large-scale scene generation by enabling the creation of scalable, high-quality assets
2. However, PCG faces challenges such as limited modular diversity, high expertise requirements, and challenges in managing the diverse elements and structures in complex scenes
3. Specifically, the proposed method comprises two components, PCGHub and PCGPlanner
