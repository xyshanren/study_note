 Mann-Whitney-Wilcoxon Test

Two data samples are independent if they come from distinct populations and the
samples do not affect each other. Using the Mann-Whitney-Wilcoxon Test, we
can decide whether the population distributions are identical without assuming them
to follow the normal distribution.

 Example

In the data frame column mpg of the data set mtcars, there are gas mileage data of
